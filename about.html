About

Where Truth Has a Home.

Lux Veritas is a global media and cultural studio operating across music, film, and live experiences. Founded on the belief that culture shapes reality, we create work that endures beyond cycles, platforms, and trends.

House

One house. Many rooms.

The public Lux Veritas world is organized as a living media house: records, studios, publishing, live rooms, community, and selected drops moving together without flattening the work.
